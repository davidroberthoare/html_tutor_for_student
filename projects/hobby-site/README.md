# Hobby Site

## Project Overview

Create a website about something you're passionate about—a hobby, interest, sport, game, book series, or anything you love!

## Suggested Sections

1. **Homepage** - Introduction to your hobby
2. **Why I Love It** - What makes it special
3. **Gallery** - Images related to your hobby
4. **Resources** - Links to learn more
5. **Tips for Beginners** - Share your knowledge

## Features to Include

- Header with site title
- Navigation to different sections
- Images (photos, artwork, screenshots)
- Lists (resources, tips, reasons)
- External links to related sites
- Fun, enthusiastic design that matches your topic

## Design Tips

- Choose colors that fit your topic
- Let your passion show through!
- Include lots of images
- Make it informative AND fun
- Show why YOU love this hobby

## Example Topics

- Gaming (favorite game or genre)
- Sports (team, sport you play)
- Books (series, author, genre)
- Music (band, genre, instrument)
- Art (style, artist, your own art)
- Cooking (cuisine, recipes, techniques)
- Nature (animals, plants, hiking)
- Technology (coding, gadgets, apps)
- Crafts (knitting, woodworking, etc.)

## Getting Started

1. Choose your hobby
2. Decide what sections to include
3. Gather images and write content
4. Choose colors/fonts that fit the vibe
5. Build and style!

## Stretch Goals

- Add a quiz or fun facts section
- Create a "getting started" guide
- Include embedded videos (research iframe)
- Add a gallery with multiple images
- Link to your own social media about the hobby

## Ask Copilot

- "How should I structure a fan site?"
- "What colors would work for [topic]?"
- "How do I create an image gallery?"
- "Ideas for sections about [hobby]?"

Make this site reflect YOUR enthusiasm!
